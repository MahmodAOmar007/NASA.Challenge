NASA's Space Biology program pioneers groundbreaking research into how living organisms adapt and evolve in space. Our interdisciplinary studies provide critical insights for long-duration missions and advance fundamental biological knowledge.

To understand how spaceflight affects living systems at molecular, cellular, and organismal levels, enabling safer human exploration and revealing fundamental biological principles.
